Who are We?

SOS Vocational Training Centre is an educational institution focused on providing practical, hands-on training and skills development in various trades such as ICT, Electrical Installation and Electronics amoung many More trades. SOS Vocational Training Centre aims to equip students with the technical skills and knowledge required for employment in various industries. Our Vocational training is typically career-oriented and is designed to prepare individuals for specific roles in sectors such as construction, manufacturing, hospitality, information technology, and more.


Our Vision

To empower young people with skills, knowledge and Values for self-reliance through Technical and Vocational skills and Training

Our Mission

To provide Quality Technical and Vocational skills through Formal and Informal training in collaboration with the community to instill ownership and responsibility

Our Objectives

  1. To provide innovative skills and experiences toyoung people to enhence their employability
  2. To develop positive attitude and behavior towards work and life among students
  3. to encourage entrepreneurial knowledge and skills and self-employement
  4. To promote Formal and Informal Vocational skills among young people for self-reliance
  5. To increase access to technical education through creative training methods
